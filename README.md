This is Ozymandias, a mod designed to change Quake4 to replicate Gun Game.

===================================================================================

Features:
  - Variety of different gameplay styles
  - A new roster of different weapon capabilities

===================================================================================

How to Play:
  - Fight through Quake 4 with the added challenge of a more lucrative health and weapon management system.
     - As your health decreases, you'll gain access to different weapons that increase in power

       (Less Health = More Power)

     - As your health increases, you'll lose access to different weapons, resulting in a decrease in power

       (More Health = Less Power)
  - Progressing through the game will allow you to unlock perks that will boost your overall power and purchase temporary upgrades that can change up your gameplay

===================================================================================
